Roll With Us!

Yo, welcome to our skateboarding club for women and non-binary folks! We're all about having a rad time on our boards, helping each other get better, and making sure everyone feels welcome in the skate scene. So come shred with us!

The Kweens

The Thrash Kweens is a women and non-binary skateboarding club dedicated to promoting inclusivity in the skating community. We hold weekly meet-ups, fundraisers, and pop-up events around the city to provide access to underserved and underrepresented communities. Through our events and community-building efforts, we aim to inspire others to join us in breaking down barriers and making skating more accessible to everyone. We're committed to sharing that with as many people as possible. Whether you're a seasoned skater or just getting started, come shred with the Thrash Kweens!
